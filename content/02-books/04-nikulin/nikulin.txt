title: Recollections of the War
----
text: So many books written about WWII, so many movies shot, yet it’s rare to find a true perspective of a real soldier. Most of the ones who fought on the front were killed; those who survived not willing to recall. Nikulin was from the latter camp, although he did eventually write down what he went through to ‘get rid of oppressive thoughts.’ After 25 years of gathering dust, that book was published in 2007; (link: http://www.imwerden.info/belousenko/books/nikulin/nikulin_vojna.htm text: a book of no illusions popup: yes). – July 2015
