title: The Subject Tonight Is Love

text: As a Sufi poet, Hafiz often talks about the mystery of love in all its forms. In (link: https://www.amazon.ca/Subject-Tonight-Love-Sweet-Poems/dp/0140196234/ref=sr_1_fkmrnull_1?crid=36GCPMQ1CKCG4&keywords=the+subject+tonight+is+love&qid=1553187746&s=gateway&sprefix=the+subje%2Caps%2C196&sr=8-1-fkmrnull text: The Subject Tonight Is Love target: _blank), he explores the affectionate connection with a lover, friend, and god — all of which he uses interchangeably. Sufi love knows no limits indeed. ‘Why ever talk of miracles / when you are destined to become / infinite love.’ – March 2018
