title: Recollections of the War
----
text: So much written about WWII, yet it’s rare to find a true perspective of a soldier. Most of the ones who fought were killed; those who survived not willing to recall. Nikulin was the latter, although he did eventually write down what he went through to ‘get rid of oppressive thoughts.’ After 25 years of gathering dust, his book was published in 2007; (link: http://www.imwerden.info/belousenko/books/nikulin/nikulin_vojna.htm text: a book of no illusions target: _blank). – July 2015
